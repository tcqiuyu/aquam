What is AQUAM?

AQUAM is an oilfield water management program designed for the oil and gas operators for effectively managing their day-to-day water management operations. It has been targeted to help operators improve their water management while simultanrously mitigating environmental impacts, while addressing the overall goal, which would be to comprehensively reduce fresh water use, optimize produced water recycling, minimizing truck traffic and other potential environmental and community concerns. It is based on a Desktop-to-Drill Site (D2D) Platform currently being developed by the CEWS.

Scientific Water Management

Oilfield water is managed in a scientific way. Here provides two ways for the water management - the traditional way based on the mathematical modeling, statitical analysis and charting, as well as a modern way based on geoanalytical techniques.

Mathematics and Statistics based Solutions

Here we design many statistical and mathematical methods used for oilfield water management. Statistical analysis, mathematical modeling are based on powerful scientific computation packages - Scipy and Numpy.

Geographic Information Science based Solutions

GIS provides an new way to tell the story behind the spatial data. It also help to visualize the data. Here we use Arcgis API for javascript and GeoDjango to support the geospatial analysis
