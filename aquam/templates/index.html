AQUAM

AQUAM is an oilfield water management program designed for the oil and gas operators for effectively managing their day-to-day water management operations. It has been targeted to help operators improve their water management while simultanrously mitigating environmental impacts, while addressing the overall goal, which would be to comprehensively reduce fresh water use, optimize produced water recycling, minimizing truck traffic and other potential environmental and community concerns. It is based on a Desktop-to-Drill Site (D2D) Platform currently being developed by the CEWS.
